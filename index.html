Sebastian Loeschcke

I am a Master student at Aarhus University specialising in Machine Learning and Data-Intensive Systems.
My research interests are in the cross section between Computer Vision and Deep Learning, especially self-supervised learning and few-shot learning. I am also interested in data visualization and methods for visual analysis.

Research


Text-Driven Stylization of Video Objects
Sebastian Loeschcke, Sagie Benaim, Serge Belongie
arXiv, 2022. project page / arXiv

We propose a framework for stylizing objects in videos using a natural language text prompt.



Progressive Parameter Space Visualization for Task-Driven SAX Configuration
Sebastian Loeschcke, Marius Hogräfer, Hans-Jörg Schulz
EuroVA, 2020. article

We propose a task-driven parametrization of PAA and SAX through a parameter space visualization that shows the difference of progressively running a given analytic computation on the original and on the reduced data for a representative set of data samples.
